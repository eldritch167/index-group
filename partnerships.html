Partnerships & Sponsorships

Sponsorship of Index Group platforms is an act of stewardship — supporting institutional infrastructure that benefits Botswana's entire corporate ecosystem.

Position Sponsorship as Stewardship

Index Group sponsors do not buy rankings. They support the operational infrastructure that makes credible recognition possible: evaluation systems, advisory coordination, data validation, archival maintenance, and platform development.

Sponsorship aligns your organization with governance standards and long-term institutional thinking. It signals a commitment to raising corporate excellence benchmarks across Botswana.

No Influence Over Rankings

Sponsorship does not influence evaluation outcomes. Organizations that sponsor Index Group are evaluated using the same methodology and standards as non-sponsors.

This firewall is enforced through independent advisory oversight, conflict-of-interest declarations, and removal clauses. Sponsors support the platform, not their own rankings.
