Index Group Overview

What Index Group Is

Index Group is a platform, not an event company. It serves as the parent structure for a family of national indexes in Botswana, providing a unified framework for corporate excellence recognition.

Why It Exists

The landscape of corporate recognition in Botswana has long been fragmented — disparate awards, inconsistent standards, and limited comparability. Index Group exists to address this by creating a single, authoritative, governance-first standard that enables meaningful evaluation and benchmarking across sectors and over time.

Long-Term Vision

Index Group is built with a decade-scale, archival mindset. It is not a short-term initiative or annual ceremony. The platform is designed for permanence: annual cycles, historical data preservation, continuous refinement, and institutional legacy-building in Botswana’s corporate ecosystem.

Governance-First Positioning

At its core, Index Group prioritises governance above all else. Recognition is earned through demonstrated integrity, transparency, leadership maturity, and institutional strength — never through sponsorship, marketing, or relationships. This governance-first approach ensures credibility, independence, and long-term trust among stakeholders, regulators, investors, and the public.
