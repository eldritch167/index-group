What Index Group Is

Parent platform for national indexes

Index Group serves as the parent platform for a family of national indexes in Botswana. It establishes a unified, governance-first framework that enables consistent, data-led evaluation of corporate performance across sectors and over time.

Builder of governance-grade recognition systems

The platform acts as a builder of governance-grade recognition systems. It develops rigorous, transparent methodologies that reflect Botswana’s unique operating context while aligning with international best practices in corporate accountability and institutional maturity.

Bridge between local institutions and global standards

Index Group functions as a deliberate bridge between local institutions and global standards. It facilitates credible comparability, fosters continuous improvement, and positions Botswana’s leading organisations within broader continental and international benchmarks without compromising local relevance.
